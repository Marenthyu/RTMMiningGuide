RTM basic Mining Guide

Required Reading

Welcome! I assume you are here to get into Raptoreum Mining. You are free to just skip to the steps to get going, but don't blame me or come asking about things explained here. Be sure to understand what you're dealing with - Cryptocurrencies are volatile and you can lose your investment. This is not financial or legal advice. Contact a finance person or lawyer for that.

Long Story Short: You can use your Computer Power to get virtual Money. Raptoreum specifically is best mined with CPUs that have a large L3 Cache with GPUs being way less efficient.

About Randomness: GhostRider (the algorithm used for Raptoreum) is also random at its core - This means you will not get a stable hashrate. Your hashrate may be lower than expected.

Old CPUs won't give you much back, so I recommend against going with anything older than 4 years or lower-end CPUs.

Getting Started

First Things first: You will need a Wallet to store your earnings. This can be done online or locally. I highly recommend a local Wallet to keep your stuff private. This Guide will show Raptoreum Core. Go ahead and Download it from GitHub. (Assuming you are on windows, you will want the file named raptoreum-x.x.x.x-win64-setup.exe - Start it after downloading and follow the installation Wizard.)

Once completed, open "Raptoreum Core" - your Wallet. It will then ask you where to store the Blockhain information. It will then go ahead and synchronize the blockchain to your computer - This will take a long time. This is not yet the mining process. It will look like this:

You can click on Hide to already use the application, but you will not see any up-to-date info about the Blockchain before it is fully synced. At the time of writing, the initial Sync for me took about 5 hours. You may be interested in using the provided bootstrap.zip File to finish quicker, but I won't explain that here. For now, let's move on.

Your receiving Address

By starting up Raptoreum Core, the app will have generated a default wallet. You can (and should) encrypt it with the Settings Menu, but we can also continue without it.

Click on File, then Receiving Addresses.... This is where you can see your Addresses you can use to Receive RTM. Click on New in the bottom left, enter any Label you would like (for example "FlockPool Mining") and click OK. Congratulations! You now have a receiving Address you can use for Mining. Write it down somewhere!

The Pool

You could now start mining with the Wallet itself, but it will be very inefficient and you most likely won't solve a block on your own. Thus, choose a Mining Pool of your choice. I recommend FlockPool. Click on Start Mining on their Website, enter your Receiving Address, any arbitrary Worker Name you want as well as an optional Password for this Pool. This Password is used to change your minimum Payout Threshold later. Click on the Copy Icon and note down the generated config. You'll need it in a bit.

The Miner

Now you just need your Mining Software - if you want to squeeze out every last penny, go compile it yourself and find one that fits you perfect; Otherwise, just go here and download the cpuminer-gr-x.x.x.x-x86_64_windows.7z File. Your Webbrowser will most likely warn you that the file is dangerous - this is technically correct as mining software is often abused. But since that is what we want, you can accept the risk. In Chrome, Open All Downloads by Pressing CTRL+J, then selecting to keep the File Anyway and confirming the secondary warning.

Once the file is downloaded, Extract it using an App like 7zip. Open Up the config.json File in a Text Editor like Notepad++ and replace the contents with the configuration you generated above. You can also toy with the other options if you want to. Save the file and close Notepad++.

Congratulations! You are now ready to Mine! Just launch the cpuminer.bat file as Administrator (right-click it, then select Run as Administrator)!

NOTICE: The first time you start the Miner, it will tune itself to your specific setup for about 2 and a half hours. It may take a while, but is worth it to optimize your Mining Efforts! Do NOT interrupt this process or it will start from scratch!

After tuning completes, mining will start. The tuning will not need to be redone once finished, unless you delete anything.

After a few minutes of Mining, you can head to your Pool's Website, enter your Receiving Address and see your progress - with FlockPool, it should show after about 30 seconds of mining or when you get your first share. FlockPool will then send you your payment twice a day at 8AM and 8PM UTC, if you are above the Payout Threshold that you can change with the password you set.

Happy Mining!
